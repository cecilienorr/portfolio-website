silfen

In our 2nd semester, we worked with our first big client: SILFEN. In this project, we had to create a SoMe strategy and SoMe content.
To solve this we took a look at the different platforms SILFEN operates on. We also took a look at other things like purpose, relevance, tone of voice and SoMe presence.

Wireframes
In this project, we worked a lot with both High Fidelity and Low Fidelity wireframes as we were to redesign SILFENs entire web solution along with their visual identity.

Content Creation
This project also included creating content for SILFENs Social Media platforms. We made a video production with a guide on what to post more of in the future.
