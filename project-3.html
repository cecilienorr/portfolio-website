green wardrobe club

Green Wardrobe Club is a start up company that I worked with for my 2nd semester exam with my amazing team!

On this project we had a lot of ground to cover so we all agreed to work on different things.
My primary tasks were the documentation, prototyping and styletile.

The whole project consisted of: SoMe strategy, SoMe content, sender-, market- and user research, coding, databases and making it user friendly.

A newer element that was a big part of our work with Green Wardrobe Club was a Business Canvas Model. It gave us a better understanding of the company and where to bring our focus.

We had to, like our previous project with SILFEN, create a SoMe video that my team and I decided to add to our frontpage.
